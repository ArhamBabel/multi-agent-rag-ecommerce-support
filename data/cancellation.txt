Orders can be cancelled before shipment confirmation unless marked as non-cancellable.

Once the order is shipped, cancellation requests may not be accepted.

Refunds for cancelled orders will be processed to the original payment method within standard timelines.

Cash on Delivery (COD) orders can be cancelled anytime before dispatch without charges.

Cancellation requests due to “change of mind” may not be allowed for certain products.

Orders placed during sales or promotions may have restricted cancellation options.

If a seller cancels the order, a full refund will be issued to the customer.

Repeated order cancellations by customers may lead to account restrictions.

Cancellation is not allowed for personalized, perishable, or made-to-order items.

If payment is successful but cancellation fails, the order will be processed or refunded as per policy.

Partial cancellation may not be allowed for bundled or combo products.

Refund timelines may vary depending on the payment method and banking partner.

Cancellation requests after dispatch refusal may incur shipping or handling charges.

Orders in processing may be cancelled, but once packed or handed to courier, cancellation may not be possible.

If a cancellation request is placed but the order is already shipped, the customer may need to initiate a return after delivery.
