Orders are typically shipped within 1–3 business days unless otherwise specified.

If a package is confirmed lost in transit, the customer is eligible for a full refund or replacement.

Late deliveries are not eligible for compensation unless explicitly stated in promotional offers.

Delivery timelines may vary based on location, courier availability, and external factors.

Customers will receive tracking details once the order is shipped.

Delays caused by weather, strikes, or unforeseen events are not under platform control.

Incorrect or incomplete shipping addresses may lead to delays or failed delivery.

Orders marked as delivered by the courier will be considered completed unless reported within 24 hours.

Multiple items in an order may be shipped separately depending on availability.

Cash on Delivery (COD) orders may require confirmation before shipment.

Shipping charges, if applicable, are non-refundable unless the error is from the platform.

Delivery attempts will be made a limited number of times; failure may result in order cancellation.

Customers must ensure availability to receive the order at the provided address.

International shipments may be subject to customs duties and additional charges.

High-value orders may require OTP or signature verification upon delivery.

Fraudulent claims related to non-delivery may result in account restrictions.
