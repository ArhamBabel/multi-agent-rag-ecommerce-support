Items marked as "final sale" are not eligible for return or refund unless defective.

Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days after approval.

Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery unless explicitly specified otherwise in the product listing.

Items must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging to be eligible for return.

Products marked as “non-returnable” cannot be returned except in case of defects or damage.

Perishable, personalized, or hygiene-sensitive items are not eligible for return unless defective.

Customers must provide photo or video evidence for damaged, defective, or incorrect items.

Return requests may be rejected if the product shows signs of use or tampering.

Pickup attempts will be made a limited number of times; failure may lead to cancellation of return.

Refunds will be processed only after the returned item passes quality inspection.

Shipping charges may be non-refundable unless the return is due to seller or platform error.

Replacement is subject to product availability; otherwise, a refund will be issued.

Returns initiated due to “change of mind” may incur return shipping or restocking fees.

Items returned with missing accessories or parts may be partially refunded or rejected.

Excessive or fraudulent return behavior may result in account restrictions or return denial.
