If a customer receives a damaged item, they must report it within 48 hours of delivery.

Missing items must be reported within 24 hours.

Customers may be required to provide photo evidence.

If the issue is verified, a refund or replacement will be issued.

False claims may result in account restrictions.

Payment failures where the amount is deducted must be reported within 24 hours with transaction details.

The payment will be verified, and a refund will be initiated if the issue is confirmed.

Fraudulent or duplicate claims may result in account restrictions.
